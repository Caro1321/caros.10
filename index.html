Homemade cotton candy

~ famous for its delicous sweetness and colors ~

~How to make homemade cotton candy~

~ingredients~

~2 1/4 cups white granualted sugar

~1/2 cup water ~cup corn syrup ~1 pinch of salt

~food coloring (optional) ~nostick cooking spray

~preparation~

1. cover your working surface with a large plastic wrap or tarp. it does get messy!

2. spray a wire cooling rack lightly with the nonstick cooking spray

3. in a saucepan over medium heat, combine sugar, corn syrup, water, and salt.

4. sitr until sugar is melted!

5. using a candy thermometer measure the liquids temp. Heat to 320 degrees.

6. once liquid reaches the temp pour the sugar into a heat-resistent bowl.

7. add food coloring or flavor extract and stir with a whisk.

8. working quickly dip the special whsik inot hte molten sugar syrup.

9. drizzle it back and forth letting small wisps of the liquid fall down

10. reapeat! until you have several starnds that begin to form a nest of sugar

11. gather the wisps in your hand and form a little ball around a lollipop stick

12. all done! sereve immediately!
